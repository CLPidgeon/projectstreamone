Welcome to the website of the wonderful Birding Group of Sale, we are a group of lovely bird watchers, find out more about us by going to our about page.

We meet every Friday and rotate through a number of different sites - check out the event page for where we've been and where we have yet to go!

We take many pictures of the birds we see, some of which can be seen here . Also on the page is the bird song of some common birds for you to listen to, this helps us identify the birds. There is also a video of some birds from one of our meetings.

If you are interested in joining, why not have a quick try at our bird guessing game? See if you can get it right without cheating!

We also have a weekly round up newsletter which goes out after each meeting. You can sign up to receive our newsletter even if you can't attend our meetings.
